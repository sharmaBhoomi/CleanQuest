Recycling Process

Our recycling workflow follows a streamlined path from collection to pelletization, ensuring every plastic product is effectively repurposed with minimal waste.

Collection & Sorting

Plastic waste is collected and sorted by type for further processing.

Shredding & Cleaning

Collected materials are shredded and cleaned to remove impurities.

Drying & Agglomeration

Moisture-free flakes are processed in agglomerators for uniformity.

Extrusion & Pelletizing

Molten plastic is extruded into pellets that serve as raw materials for manufacturing.
